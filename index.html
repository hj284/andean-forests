The Forests of the Andes: A Cascade of Life

The Andes of South America contain the greatest levels of biodiversity on the planet. This site showcases the rich and complex soundscapes of the region where the cool wet cloud forests meet the tropical jungles of Amazonia, allowing you to immerse yourself in pure and lush environments.
